Creativity and security in Automation of Business Processes

The National Software Developers Hackerton brings together the community of experts, academics, and activists who comprise the Creative Commons affiliate network in a different country every two years. This year, we hope to expand our invitation list including organizations and invidivuals who want to work with us on shared projects that advance the cause of the Commons, free culture and open knowledge.
